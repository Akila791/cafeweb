About The Gallery Café

Welcome to The Gallery Café, where art meets cuisine. Located in the heart of Colombo, our café offers a unique dining experience inspired by art and culture. Established in 2020, we have been serving delicious meals crafted with the finest ingredients, catering to a diverse palate with our extensive menu.

At The Gallery Café, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our menu features a variety of dishes, from traditional Sri Lankan favorites to contemporary culinary creations from Chinese and Italian cuisines, all prepared with love and attention to detail. Whether you're here for a casual meal, a special occasion, or to enjoy our art exhibitions, we strive to make your visit memorable.

We are committed to sustainability and use locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. Our friendly staff and cozy ambiance make The Gallery Café the perfect spot for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or a coffee break. Join us and discover the perfect blend of art and gastronomy.

Our vision is to create a space where culinary art and visual art coexist, providing a holistic experience that delights all the senses. Our mission is to offer exceptional food and service while supporting local artists and fostering a community around the love of food and art.
